AssureMusic

Info:

Assure-Music is a bot, fully musical.
It uses the DisTube package, and is much simpler than AssureBOT.

Commands:

> Normal Commands:


- help - shows all bot commands
- bot-info (bot, botinfo) - shows info about bot
- calculator (calc) - calculate eclarifies the action given
- avatar (pfp) - shows a pinged user avatar
- ping (ms) - shows a bot latency in ms

> Config Commands:


- setprefix - changes bot prefix

> Music Commands:


- play (p) - plays music
- stop - stopps the music (and disconnects from the voice channel)
- pause - pausing music
- resume - resuming music
- skip - skipps music
- queue - shows music queue
- loop - loops song/queue
- volume - changes music volume


So now, you know all Assure-Music Commands. Enjoy!
